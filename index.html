Our Cafes

Roastery Coffee House has outlets across multiple cities — Hyderabad, Kolkata, Delhi, Noida, Lucknow, Jaipur. Find a cafe near your location to enjoy India's finest coffees in spaces designed meticulously to elevate your coffee experience.

Become a part of our design ideology — Modern Indian Minimalism. Our cafes are aesthetic and accented with Indian handmade accessories yet we are rooted in practicality, sustainability, and simplicity.

Why Roastery?

INDIA'S FINEST COFFEES

We brew only India's finest coffee beans available. All our coffees have been awarded a score of 80 and above

ROASTED FRESH

We brew only freshly roasted beans. Our beans are roasted under expert supervision and care using state-of-the-art equipment in our own Roasting units.

RESPONSIBLY SOURCED

We practice Responsible and Ethical sourcing of beans. This helps us preserve the environment, and keep the quality of coffee beans intact.
